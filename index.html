Yo! I'm Alex.


I'm an Americian solo female traveller living out of a backpack since 2016, travelling to all kind of places many tourist never consider. cultures call to me, maps lie to me, and i'll go anywhere so long as there are friendly faces and wild spaces.

My goal is to inspire you to question stereotypes, travel responsibly... and see how girls can travel anywhere.
